# Curtido

![Recipe Image](../../public/assets/curtido.jpg)

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
|------------|----------|
|Distilled Vinegar | 1/4 cup |
|Sugar | 1 1/2 tsp |
|Chili Flakes | To Taste |
|Oregono | 1 tsp|
|Carrot | 1 Lg |
|Onion | 1/2 onion|
|Cabbage | 1/4 head| 
Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients, season with salt, toss and chill
